iOSGods iAP Cracker is a tweak that is similar to the well known LocaliApStore

    Free iAP Purchase for some apps and games, it doesn’t always work so don’t expect much. Turn on the tweak inside iOS Settings. This tweak is made by @Laxus, supported A12 / A13 devices.

NOTE: This doesn’t work on all games and apps
